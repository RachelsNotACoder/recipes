Foil Packet North African Spiced Fish and Couscous

Prep / Total 20m / 35m
Tags
Mixed African White Fish
Servings
6

Oregano and caraway-spiced couscous and vegetables steam beneath flaky white fish in foil packets, adapted from a Food Network classic.

Ingredients

Preparation

  1. 01

    Preheat your oven to 425 degrees, or your outdoor grill with plenty of room for cooking on indirect heat.

  2. 02

    Read the preparation directions for your couscous, and prepare using just half of the liquid called for.

  3. 03

    Combine all the spices together.

  4. 04

    Combine the partly prepared couscous with the diced vegetables and about half the spice mixture.

  5. 05

    Divide fish and couscous-vegetable mixture into six portions, and wrap in foil packets with the couscous on the bottom and the fish on the top. Sprinkle the top with the remaining spice mixture.

  6. 06

    Put sealed foil packets in preheated oven on a baking sheet or grill and cook for 10-14 minutes. For cod -- a fairly dense, firm fish -- fillets that are about 6 oz each, cook time should be 15 minutes. More delicate fish like sole or tilapia, or smaller, thinner fillets, might be done a bit faster.

Tips and Notes

  • This recipe is adapted from the Food Network. Not everyone has harissa in their kitchens though, so I'm advising using a small amount of chili powder instead. If you use cayenne, I'd suggest using even less unless you like it really spicy! I'm also giving a technique for partially cooking the couscous right in the foil packets to soak up the juices that the vegetables will release in the foil packet, making the whole thing less messy overall.
