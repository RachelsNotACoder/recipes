Chicken Chili Stuffed Sweet Potato

Prep / Total 15m / 1h 15m
Tags
Mixed SW Chicken
Servings
6

Smoky ground chicken chili with black beans and corn, piled into a roasted sweet potato for a hearty, no-fuss dinner.

Ingredients

Preparation

  1. 01

    Make the chili first. Preheat a dutch oven on medium high with the oil and brown the chicken in it, about 3-5 minutes.

  2. 02

    Add the onion and bell pepper and continue cooking until softened and beginning to release liquid, 3-5 more minutes.

  3. 03

    Stir in the garlic and spices until fragrant, then stir in the tomatoes, beans, and soy sauce. Bring to a simmer then reduce the heat to maintain a low simmer for an hour. Stir in the frozen corn for just the last 10 minutes to thaw and heat through.

  4. 04

    Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Prick sweet potatoes several times with a fork and place in the oven to roast for 40-60 minutes (time will depend on size of potatoes), until tender. (To cook more quickly, you can microwave the potatoes for about 90 seconds first, then roast for about half the time).

  5. 05

    If you used large potatoes, cut in half lengthwise before serving. Otherwise, serve each potato whole with a slit on top. The chili can be served right inside (and spilling over) the sweet potato, or on the side.

Tips and Notes

  • I used to have a favorite lunch place, now closed, that sold takeaway baked potatoes that you could optionally add chili to. It was delicious. I happen to love sweet potatoes with southwest flavors, so I wanted to try the same idea with them and I think it worked out great.
  • You can make the chili in advance (it's great on its own) and just heat up to serve with the baked sweet potatoes, or you can just start baking the potatoes at the same time the chili sets to simmering.
