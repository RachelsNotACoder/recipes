Tofu W'at

Prep / Total 15m / 45m
Tags
Saucepan African Tofu
Servings
4

Berbere-braised tofu in the tradition of Doro W'at — same bold spice foundation, same deep color, swapping chicken for tofu with results that hold up beautifully.

Ingredients

Preparation

  1. 01

    Place the onion, berbere, cardamom, garlic powder, ginger, fenugreek, and butter or oil in a nonstick wok or large skillet. Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until the onions have softened, about 5–7 minutes.

  2. 02

    Add the tofu and vegetable broth and stir until everything is well combined. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium.

  3. 03

    Cook uncovered for 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. The sauce is ready when it clings to the back of a wooden spoon. Season with salt to taste — some berbere blends already contain salt, so taste before adding.

Tips and Notes

  • Berbere varies a lot by brand — some are saltier or hotter than others. Taste yours before you start and adjust accordingly. If you can find it, a good store-bought berbere is the biggest flavor lever in this dish.
  • Great served over rice or with flatbread to soak up the sauce.
